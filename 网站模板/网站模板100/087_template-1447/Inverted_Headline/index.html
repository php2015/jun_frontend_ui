Posted on 2/9/2008 at 08:53:27GMTA New Template from DemusDesign

Welcome to Inverted Headline, the tenth template from DemusDesign. I started out thinking of a high-contrast, light on dark, newspaper-style template, and ended up with this. I figured we could always use more dark templates out there. Nothing revolutionary here, just a clean, tight, dark template.

I release it under a Creative Commons License, which means you are free to use it and change it in anyway, so long as you include the link to my site in the footer. If you do use it, send me an email through my site and let me know. I love seeing what people doe with my work. Thanks and enjoy.
