About this...

This is my entry for the 'Spring' design competition. I have chose this design as to make it stand out, and also make it big (or small) enough to fit onto portable devices (namely the iPod Touch or iPhone.)

With this design, more or less everything can be customised to suit. The header can be changed, fonts, layouts, the power of CSS can never cease to amaze.
